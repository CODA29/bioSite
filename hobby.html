"A picture is worth a thousand words"

I dedicated this space to showcasing hobbies that bring my brother joy and creativity! Naod has always been the kind of person who dives headfirst into his passions, turning everyday moments into extraordinary adventures that inspire us all. These days, most of his activities are all about spending time with his daughter. He often says that she's the one who has taught him so much since she came into his life! Among my siblings, he is known for having good eyes for art. Painting takes the most significant part of the things he loves to do in his leisure time. His works are inspired by the stories he grew up hearing and the absurdity he finds in what people consider normal. He is a passionate fan of anime and cartoon characters, which are common elements in some of his paintings. The following are some of the paintings he has created recently.
